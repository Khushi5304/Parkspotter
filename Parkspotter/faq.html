Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What is ParkSpotter?

ParkSpotter is a website for finding and reserving parking spots in various locations. It helps users save time and hassle by providing information about available parking spots and allowing them to book spots in advance.

Question 2: How do I reserve a parking spot on ParkSpotter?

To reserve a parking spot on ParkSpotter, simply search for your desired location and select an available spot from the list. Then, follow the prompts to complete the reservation process and make your payment.

Question 3: Is there a fee for using ParkSpotter?

ParkSpotter is free to use for searching and browsing available parking spots. However, there may be fees associated with reserving a parking spot, which will be displayed during the reservation process.

Question 4: Can I cancel my parking reservation?

Yes, you can cancel your parking reservation on ParkSpotter. Simply log in to your account, navigate to your reservations, and follow the prompts to cancel your reservation. Please note that cancellation policies may vary depending on the parking provider.
